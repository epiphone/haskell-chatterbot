main Sentence Types:
S   -> NP VP              Declaratives: A plane left.
S   -> VP                 Imperatives: Leave! Show the lowest fare.

yes-no-questions:
S   -> VQ NP               Does she? Would You?
S   -> VQ NP VP            Did the plane leave?
S   -> VQ NP VP PP         Would you go there in the morning? (V turha, koska verb-luokkaa ei ikinä)

wh-questions:
S   -> WH aux NP          What is your name?
S   -> WH aux NP VP       How much does it cost?
S   -> WH NP VP           WH-subject Questions: What airlines fly from Burbank to Denver?
S   -> WH NP aux NP VP    WH non-subject Questions: what flight do you have from Burbank?

WH  -> wh | wh det         How, how many

NP  -> NOM | det NOM
